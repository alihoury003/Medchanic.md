Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Medchanic.md ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (encrypted and stored securely via Appwrite). We also store your test history, question usage, and performance data to power the dashboard and progress tracking features.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To track your study progress and show performance analytics
  • To send you password reset emails when requested
  • To improve our question bank and platform based on usage patterns

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Appwrite's cloud infrastructure with industry-standard encryption. Passwords are hashed using Argon2 and are never stored in plain text. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to maintain your session and study preferences. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at medchanicmd@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Medchanic.md is intended for medical students and healthcare professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 18.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes via email. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at medchanicmd@gmail.com.
