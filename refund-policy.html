Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Refund Policy explains when paid Medchanic.md subscriptions may be eligible for a refund. Because Medchanic.md provides instant access to digital educational content, refunds are limited and reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

1. Free Access Before Payment

Medchanic.md offers limited free access so users can try the platform before subscribing. We encourage users to explore the free question blocks before purchasing paid access.

2. Monthly Plan Refunds

Monthly subscription purchases may be eligible for a refund if requested within 48 hours of purchase and if the account has not shown substantial use of paid content.

3. 3-Month and Annual Plan Refunds

Three-month and annual plan purchases may be eligible for a refund if requested within 7 days of purchase and if the account has not shown substantial use of paid content.

4. Substantial Use

Refund requests may be declined if the account has completed or accessed a significant number of paid questions, used the platform extensively, or otherwise received substantial value from the subscription.

5. Renewals

Renewal payments may be eligible for review if requested within 48 hours of the renewal charge and if no meaningful platform use occurred after the renewal date. Users are responsible for managing or cancelling subscriptions before renewal.

6. Promo Codes and Founder Discounts

Promo codes and founder discounts are limited-use offers. Refunds for discounted purchases are calculated based on the actual amount paid, not the original undiscounted price.

7. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Requests made outside the refund window
  • Accounts with substantial paid content usage
  • Account sharing, abuse, scraping, or violation of the Terms of Use
  • Purchases made with incorrect expectations after free access was available

8. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email medchanicmd@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and include the email address used for your Medchanic.md account, purchase date, plan purchased, and reason for the request.

9. Processing Time

If a refund is approved, processing time depends on the payment provider and the user’s bank or card issuer. Refunds may take several business days to appear.

10. Educational Disclaimer

Medchanic.md is an independent educational exam-preparation resource. We do not guarantee specific exam outcomes, score improvements, or admission results.

11. Contact
